Frequently asked questions

What are credits? +

Credits are units of computation used by our AI. On average, generating a complete 10-slide presentation uses approximately 100-200 credits, depending on complexity and content length.

What happens if I run out of credits? +

You can easily purchase additional credit packs or upgrade to a higher tier. We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit so you're never caught off guard.

Can I change plans at any time? +

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing adjustments.

Do credits roll over? +

Unused credits expire at the end of each monthly billing period. We recommend choosing a plan that matches your typical usage to maximize value.

Is there a free trial? +

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with 500 credits to test all features. No credit card required to start.
