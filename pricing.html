Pricing FAQ

Common questions

What are tokens?

Tokens are units of computation used by our AI. On average, generating a complete 10-slide presentation uses approximately 1-2M tokens, depending on complexity and content length.

What happens if I run out of tokens?

You can easily purchase additional token packs or upgrade to a higher tier. We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit so you're never caught off guard.

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing adjustments.

Do tokens roll over?

Unused tokens expire at the end of each monthly billing period. We recommend choosing a plan that matches your typical usage to maximize value.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with 5M tokens to test all features. No credit card required to start.
