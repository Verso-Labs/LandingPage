Frequently asked questions

How does VersoAI match my brand?

VersoAI reads your existing PowerPoint slidemasters, themes, and color palettes. Every generated slide uses your fonts, layouts, and brand colors — so new slides are indistinguishable from ones you made yourself.

Do I need to install anything?

The web chat works in any browser — no installation needed. The PowerPoint add-in is a one-click install from the Office Add-ins store.

Is my data secure?

Yes. VersoAI is SOC 2 compliant with end-to-end encryption. We never train on your data, and all files are deleted after processing unless you choose to save them.

Can I edit the generated slides?

Absolutely. Every slide is a fully editable native PowerPoint element. Move shapes, change text, adjust layouts — it's your deck.

What file formats are supported?

VersoAI outputs standard .pptx files that work in PowerPoint, Google Slides, and Keynote. You can also export to PDF.
